~ Crystals Collector ~

You will be given a Target Number at the start of each game. You win the game by matching Your Number with the Target Number. Increase Your Number by clicking on each of the four crystals. If Your Number goes above the Target Number --- you lose. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time you start a new game, the crystals will have new hidden values.
